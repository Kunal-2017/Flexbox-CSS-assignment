QUE.2 Explain the role of following key properties in the Flexbox layout model.

Sol.
1. Justify-Content: It is used to allign items and to give space between items along main(x) axis.
Ex- Justify-content: flex-start,space-between,centre etc.

2. Allign-items: It is used to allign items and to give space between items along cross(y) axis.
Ex- allign-items: flex-start,space-between,centre etc.

3. Gap: It is used to space between items.

4.Flex-direction: It is used to dispaly items along rows or columns.
    Ex-Flex-direction:rows or columns

5.Flex-wrap: It is used to display items in one rows or more than one rows.
